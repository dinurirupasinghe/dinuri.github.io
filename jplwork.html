NASA JPL Small Satellite Dynamics Testbed Research Project

Results: June 2023 to August 2023



I was a Guidance and Control (Section 343) intern at NASA JPL. I modeled satellite attitude control for novel alkane propulsion payload using the JPL Small Satellite Dynamics Testbed (SSDT) in Matlab and Simulink, applied feedforward and feedback control principles for optimization. I also designed and built multiuse hardware for the SSDT and dynamometer testbed in solidworks and performed stress analysis and inertia analysis as a function of changing dimensions and materials of hardware.

Results:

At the culmination of the internship, I had generated a complete CAD model of our hardware model, a bill of materials, and a working simulation of the system.
